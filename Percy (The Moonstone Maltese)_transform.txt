DEFINITION: A black Maltese mix with celestial magic, Percy is Celestial Academy's reluctant hero whose defensive powers shine brightest when protecting others—except from Pippa's puppy eyes.

DESCRIPTION:
Percy's jet-black fur ripples with silver undertones, as if someone combed starlight through his coat. The moonstone at his throat casts shifting constellations across his chest, pulsing violently whenever Malyster's shadow passes nearby. His **Paw Protection** manifests as iridescent domes of energy—perfect for shielding allies from dragonfire but useless against his little "sister's" manipulative whimpers. The real wild card? His **Treat Transfiguration**: attempt a healing biscuit, and you'll get a sentient gingerbread monstrosity demanding union rights. 

Beneath his brave facade, Percy fears his magic isn't truly his—the moonstone belonged to a fallen guardian whose whispered warnings haunt his dreams. He trains obsessively to prove he's worthy, though his spells tend to backfire spectacularly (last week's "simple levitation" resulted in the astronomy tower orbiting Neptune). 

His greatest secret? Pippa isn't his sister. She's his *daughter* from a botched time spell, though neither remembers the incident that scattered her stardust across his timeline.
